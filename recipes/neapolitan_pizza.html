Neapolitan Pizza

Home

Description

This is a classic Neapolitan pizza waiting to take you on a journey to the beautiful town of Naples , Italy. Made with crushed tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, olive oil, and basil. Its baked at a high temperature and usually served hot as a main course meal .Its red , white and green cololors represent the Italian flag.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour (or all-purpose flour)
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup crushed San Marzano tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil for drizzling

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover and let the dough rise for 1 to 2 hours or until doubled.
  5. Preheat your oven as hot as it can go (450 to 500 F / 230to260 C).
  6. Shape the dough into a thin round, pushing from the center outward.
  7. Spread the crushed tomatoes over the dough.
  8. Add pieces of fresh mozzarella and a few basil leaves.
  9. Drizzle lightly with olive oil.
  10. Bake until the crust is puffed and slightly charred, 7to10 minutes.
